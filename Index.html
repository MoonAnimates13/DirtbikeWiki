2-Stroke vs 4-Stroke Engines

2-Stroke

Lighter, louder, quicker power burst. Used in racing due to rapid acceleration and fewer moving parts.

4-Stroke

More stable power, longer engine life, better for trail and enduro. Cleaner emissions and more fuel efficient.

10 Cool Dirt Bike Facts

1. The first motocross race was held in 1924 in Surrey, England.
2. Two-strokes dominated motocross until the late 1990s.
3. Yamaha’s YZ400F revolutionized 4-stroke racing in 1998.
4. Travis Pastrana landed the first double backflip in competition.
5. Electric dirt bikes are now competing with gas-powered models.
6. Some dirt bikes have smart tuning systems built in.
7. James Stewart was known as the “Fastest Man on the Planet.”
8. Some bikes weigh under 200 pounds dry.
9. Inverted forks changed the handling game in the 1990s.
10. The Red Bull Hare Scramble is one of the toughest races on Earth.
